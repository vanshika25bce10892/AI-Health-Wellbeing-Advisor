# AI-Health-Wellbeing-Advisor


Overview 

The AI Health and Well Being Advisor is an AI project that helps users find basic health information through an interactive program. It combines two tools into one system:

1. Body Mass Index (BMI) and Diet Advisor calculates your Body Mass Index. Gives you general dietary advice.

2. Symptom Checker asks about symptoms to find possible conditions and gives basic advice.

This project shows off Artificial Intelligence skills like rules and decision making to help people solve common health problems. It's for people who want to learn about the basics of Artificial Intelligence.

Features:

• The program calculates your Body Mass Index based on what you tell it.

• It tells you if your Body Mass Index is too low right too high or very high.

• It gives you some ideas for eating healthy and exercising.

• The program helps figure out if you might have a health problem based on your symptoms.

• It uses rules to try to determine what might be wrong with you based on your symptoms.

• It's easy to use by typing commands into the computer.

Concepts in Artificial Intelligence

This project uses concepts in Artificial Intelligence:

• Rule-Based System: The program follows rules to give people advice based on their Body Mass Index.

• Decision Tree Logic: The program looks at all the symptoms you tell it and tries to figure out what might be wrong with you.

• Basic Intelligent Agent Interaction: The program talks to people. Gives them advice based on what they tell it.
